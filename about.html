Get to know about our company

Artistic Village brings together a team of dedicated and professional creatives under one roof to produce state of the art productions We seek to work with our clients to ensure they achieve their communication goals

We produce corporate documentaries, short videos for social media, adverts for TV and infomercial videos

1. CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT:

Meet, discuss, pitch, brainstorm, formulate and confirm a direction and/or approach for a project. Agree on Terms and Conditions

2. PRE-PRODUCTION:

Scriptwriting, Previsualisation, Sourcing, Planning and Scheduling.

3. PRODUCTION:

Filming and Recording of scripted, planned and scheduled Content. Cast and Crew on Location.

4. DELIVERY & MARKETING:

The Mastering and Delivery of the finished Audio-Visual Production. Marketing Material released and Managed - if necessary and predetermined.

5. POST-PRODUCTION:

Editing - Cutting Down, Trimming, Compiling and Sweetening of the recorded audio-visual material
